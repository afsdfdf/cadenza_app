    About

    Cadenza — Music RWA

    On-chain Royalties NFT + ERC20 Liquidity Web3 Fans

    Cadenza turns music royalties into on-chain assets. Artists can tokenize a portion of future streaming revenue; investors gain transparent yield and liquidity. The platform bridges creators, investors, and fans with a futuristic player, online streaming, and an active market for tokenized tracks.

    • On‑chain royalty distribution
    • NFT + ERC‑20 dual‑layer assets
    • Artist funding and community participation

    1) Submit

    Upload audio and proof. We verify authenticity and rights.

    2) Tokenize

    NFT proof + ERC‑20 shares enable trading and revenue split.

    3) Earn

    Streaming income auto‑distributed to holders on‑chain.

    Technology

    Core mechanisms

    Oracle Attestation Streaming Vault Auto Payout
    • Multi‑source royalty ingestion: aggregate monthly DSP statements into an on‑chain vault.
    • Oracle attestation: verified feeds sign statements to prevent tampering and disputes.
    • Programmable distribution: pro‑rata payouts to ERC‑20 holders on a fixed schedule.
    • Compliance guard: region allow‑lists, KYC tiers and transfer limits when required.
    • Emergency switch: pause, escrow and legal review path for disputes.
    • Liquidity design: AMM pair + protocol‑owned liquidity for price stability.
    • Optional ZK proofs: protect sensitive reports while keeping verifiability.
